I love music!

I play the guitar and (try to) sing a bit too. I also listen to music a lot.

I am the guitarist in the Nepean All-City Lab Band as well as in the Merivale High School Jazz Band.

Featured Recordings

My favourite song is Jigsaw Falling Into Place, by Radiohead, my favourite band. I mostly listen to rock, pop, and jazz. Some other artists I like are John Mayer, Laufey, Blur, Olivia Rodrigo, Julian Lage, Pat Metheny, Wes Montgomery, Ella Fitzgerald, Pink Floyd, Tom Waits, Queen, and The Smile.

I have been taking guitar lessons since spring of 2019 with a local musician named Lucas Haneman. Give his band, the Lucas Haneman Express, a listen!
